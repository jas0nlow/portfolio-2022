 Outcomes

A Seamless User Experience To Grow New Customers and Impress Long-Term Partners

The new website is projected to launch in January 2026. The final product is a modern website that aligns with Fortress Technology's reputation for excellence. It provides a vastly improved user experience, making it easier for customers to find solutions and connect with the company. By focusing on clear navigation and valuable content, the new site is poised to significantly increase qualified leads, improve SEO performance, and strengthen the Fortress brand across all its global markets.

Personally, I learned how important it is to stay agile as new requests and roadblocks appear during a project. This was a lean UX project, with minimal hours to address such a large web build. I hit the ground running along with the client to turn around key deliverables week by week.
