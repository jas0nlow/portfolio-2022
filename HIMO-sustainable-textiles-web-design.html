HIMO Web Design

Canada wastes nearly 2 million tonnes of food a year. HIMO is a sustainable textiles manufacturer that turns food waste into fashion. HIMO’s name comes from the Japanese word for string or thread and the word "himochi" for the length of time which food products will keep. I was responsible for the web design and UX/UI and worked collaboratively with Geraldine Yaris (illustration) and Kat Teh (branding). See the full case study here.
