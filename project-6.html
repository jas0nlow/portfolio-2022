HIMO Sustainable textiles Web Design

Canada wastes nearly 2 million tonnes of food a year. HIMO is a sustainable textiles manufacturer that turns food waste into fashion. HIMO’s name comes from the Japanese word for string or thread and the word himochi for the length of time which food products will keep. This collaborative project was created with Geraldine (illustration) and Kat (branding).
