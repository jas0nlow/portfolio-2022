  • About me

    I’m Jason Low, a lover of loud music and loud designs. If my time spent playing puzzle and dress up flash games has taught me anything it’s that the only limit on my creativity is my bed time. I’m a contemporary designer with a lively, adaptable attitude towards UX/UI and illustration. I believe in generating deliberate, visionary work that pairs fun and logic like a burger and fries. I daydream of illustrating for children’s storybooks and novel publications and some day publishing my own story!
