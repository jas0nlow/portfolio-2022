About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary designer specializing in UX and UI, with a background in visual communication and illustration. My work is grounded in the belief that design is not just a final product, but a process of facilitation—a tool to help teams solve meaningful problems and achieve clear outcomes.

The real value I bring lies in the thoughtful, collaborative process that gets me there. I’m driven to create experiences that are both engaging and logical, grounding my work and collaborations with intention. I think critically about what matters, what works, and what doesn’t, ensuring we build things with purpose—not just out of habit.
