• About me

    I’m Jason Low, a lover of loud music and loud designs. If my time spent playing puzzle and dress up flash games has taught me anything, it’s that the only limit on my creativity is my bed time.

    I am a multidisciplinary designer with an aptitude for UX/UI and illustration. I believe in generating visionary work that pairs fun and logic like a burger and fries. I daydream of illustrating for children’s storybooks publishing my own story!
